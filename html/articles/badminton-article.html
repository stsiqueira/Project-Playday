Badminton

How to Shoot (1/2)

  1. Clear Shot – Move to the back of the court and hit an overhead shot, then move close to the net to make an underhand clear shot.
  2. Drop Shot – Move to the net with your arm shoulder high and lightly flick the racquet with aim of the shuttle just dropping over the net. Another way to make this shot is to move to the back of the court and lift the arm to hit an overhand shot.

How to Shoot (2/2)

  1. Smash Shot – Bring your racquet over your head and continue in a downwards motion at speed with the aim of making the shuttlecock hit the floor on the other side of the net.
  2. Drive Shot – This shot can be done with both forehand and backhand. Drive shots do not need a lot of movement with your feet.

Clothing and Gear

  1. Shuttlecock should be selected according to your preferred size and weight, and made of feathers or plastic.
  2. Wear comfortable athletic shoes that make it easy to take quick steps and attack.
  3. Choose lightweight athletic clothing, with sweat wicking or natural fibers.
