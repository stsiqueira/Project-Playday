Badminton

How to shoot (1/2)

1. Clear Shot - Move to the back of the court and hit an overhead shot and you will move close to the net to make the underhand clear shot.

2. Drop Shot - Move into the net with your arm shoulder high and lightly flick the racquet with aim of the shuttle just dropping over the net or Move to the back of the court and lift the arm to hit with an overhand shot.

How to shoot 2/2

3. Smash Shot - Bring your racquet over your head and continue in a downwards motion at speed with the aim of making the shuttlecock hit the floor on the other side of the net.

4. Drive Shot-This shot can be done on both forehand and backhand, with drive shots you don't really need to move your feet.

Clothing and Gear

1. Shuttlecock should be according to the perfect size and weight i.e., feather or plastic.

2. Wear shoes that make your feet easy to take steps and attack

3. Choose your clothes according to your comfort zone for playing.
