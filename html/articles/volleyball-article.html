Volleyball

How to pass a ball

  1. Keep your body's center of gravity level when moving.
  2. Form a solid platform with your forearms.
  3. Use your hips and legs to power your body upwards and connect with the ball, not just the arms.
  4. Concentrate on getting into a stable position quickly, every time you pass.

How to block

  1. Have a good view of the court.
  2. Identify the front row players.
  3. The volleyball techniques of shuffling and the crossover step are used to move into the optimal blocking position.
  4. Position the hands with the fingers opened wide, and the thumbs pointed upwards, when blocking.

Clothing and Gear

  1. Choose a professionally rated volleyball for playing on the courts or outdoor spaces.
  2. Choose the right shoes that are appropriate for the sports and comfortable for yourself.
  3. Choose lightweight, loose fitting activewear, for versatility and comfort.
