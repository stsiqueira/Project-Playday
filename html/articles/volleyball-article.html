Volleyball

How to pass a ball in volleyball

  1. Keep your body's center of gravity level when moving.
  2. Form a solid platform with your forearms.
  3. Use your hips and legs to pass, not the arms.
  4. Concentrate on getting into a stable position quickly, every time you pass.

How to block Volleyball

  1. Having a Good court vision.
  2. Identify the front row players.
  3. The volleyball techniques shuffle and crossover step are used to move into optimal blocking position.
  4. Wide fingers and thumbs up when blocking.

Clothing and Gear

  1. Choose the professional volleyball for playing in the courts or grounds.
  2. Choose the right shoes that are appropriate for the sports and comfortable for yourself.
  3. Choose the clothes according to the comfort for your zone.
