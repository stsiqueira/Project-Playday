Tennis

How to Grip a Tennis Racket

  1. Hold the racket pointing away from you with one hand.
  2. Orient the string area perpendicular to the ground.
  3. Place the base knuckle of your index finger on the long flat side of the grip facing straight out to your right.
  4. Close your hand around the grip so that this same bevel of the grip goes diagonally across your palm.

How to Shoot

  1. Stand with your feet behind the baseline and toss the tennis ball into the air.
  2. As the ball travels back down, you contact it pushing it over the net and diagonally into the service box where your opponent is.
  3. Spend a lot of time practicing this crucial stroke.

Clothing and Gear

  1. Choose a Racket for your size with tight strings. Lightweight rackets would be preferable.
  2. Tension of the string would be high to hit the ball harder.
  3. Choose a hard surface to play such as the wooden floor courts.
  4. Wear white clothes (reputation of tennis) or whichever you are preferable to make you comfortable for playing.
